Privacy Policy Your data is secure and on-device

At GingerNotes, we believe that your study materials, thoughts, and progress belong strictly to you. Our privacy commitments are straightforward:

100% Local Storage All your notes, flashcard decks, study sessions, and progress metrics are stored securely in your browser's local sandbox storage. We never upload your notes to any cloud database.
AI Summarizer Privacy Text processed through our advanced AI summarization pipes is securely analyzed in real-time. Your source texts and generated notes are never cached, saved, or used to train public models.
Complete Deletion Control You are in full control of your account. When you select "Delete Account" in Settings, our code instantly executes a full sweep of your device's memory, deleting all traces of your logs and database.
